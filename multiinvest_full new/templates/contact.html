About Us

About Multinvest

Founded in june 2013, Multinvest is a growing platform designed to bridge the gap between all investors (especially first-time investors) and trusted real estate opportunities. By combining innovation with transparency, we empower individuals to start their investment journey with confidence while connecting them to firms that deliver lasting value.

Our Mission

To deliver trusted, relationship-driven real estate services while empowering our clients to invest smarter and live better. We combine local expertise with modern technology to create a seamless, informed experience from search to close. To make investing simple, accessible, and rewarding. MustInvest is committed to helping new investors grow their wealth safely through real estate partnerships, while giving firms a platform to showcase opportunities backed by trust and professionalism.

Who We Serve

Multinvest works with first-time buyers, experienced homeowners, and luxury investors throughout the Pacific Northwest. Whether you’re searching for a downtown condo, a waterfront retreat, or a smart investment property, we provide guidance every step of the way.

Why Multinvest

With over 300 offices and a team rooted in the communities we serve, we pride ourselves on staying connected—locally and personally. Real estate isn’t just transactions to us; it’s about trust, relationships, and delivering value that lasts. We are more than just a platform—we’re a partner in your journey. With features like 5% balance growth every 30 days, secure investment tracking, and real-time firm listings, Multinvest ensures that investors have clarity, control, and confidence in their decisions. We believe investments should be simple, transparent, and rewarding. That’s why Multinvest is built around trust, technology, and long-term relationships.
